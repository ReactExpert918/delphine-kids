Our Vision

Learn More About Us, Our Work And Our Cultural Activities

We believe that learning a language can be easy and fun. Miracles happen and they are real in Delfin Center! That is why our students receive a different learning. With a choice of individual or course learning, offline or online, from level A1 to C1, for over 20 years we have been teaching children, young people and adults to practice English with full confidence! We have handed out over 10,000 certificates for successful tuition, and many of our students have successful careers in various fields.

The centre offers various activities related to foreign language courses in English, German, Italian, Spanish, Russian, Greek and Bulgarian for foreigners, as well as translation and legalization, and personal development courses.

We offer also a preparation for the Cambridge University English Certificate Levels exams, the Grade 12 English exam and the IELTS exam.

During the summer holidays from the beginning of June to the beginning of September, a Summer Day School for children from 5 to 11 years of age is held in the town of Petrich. The idea is to have fun by learning and exploring the world around us in a fun, interactive and building way, helping the personal growth of the younger generation!

In our program we have set on educational excursions for children in the region of Petrich and Bulgaria, hikes in the mountain Belasitsa together with the park Belasitsa - Petrich and two days a week beach to Rupite.

Our Goals

The aim of Delphin Language & Educational Centre is to provide quality education for our students, and to improve the living standards of the Community. We want to raise healthy, happy, self-confident personalities and neighbouring communities.

  • Our Summer School teach English to children and so improve their career opportunities.
  • Our Learning Centre to introduce young children to basic Khmer subjects, elementary English, and Health & Hygiene so they can progress confidently into government schools.
  • Our IT program to teach computing skills which improve career opportunities.
  • Our Health & Hygiene program to educate children and their families to the importance of cleanliness and good hygiene at school and in the home.
  • Our cultural activities and creative art sessions help our students to develop creative thinking and improve their communicative skills.
