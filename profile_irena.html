My mission is to encourage language development through immersion and to expand the horizons of my students.

My stimulating and dynamic lessons are adapted to all language levels and tailored according my students needs. I usually teach in small class sizes which allows me to help students individually in person or in my online classroom. I also encourage my students to take part in social activities which enable and to put their new language skills into practice.
