Contact forms

We collect the basic data that you provide to us through the contact forms. These are the name, email, message, IP data and browser type through which the contact form is sent, which is located on our “Contact” page. This data is required in order to service your requests and inquiries and at the same time to prevent etc. unwanted “spam”.

Cookies

If you leave a comment on our site, you can allow the storage of your name, email address and website in cookies. They are for your convenience, so you don’t have to fill in your details again when you leave another comment. These cookies will be kept for one year.

If you have an account and log in to this site, we will set a temporary cookie to determine if your browser accepts cookies. This cookie does not contain personal data and is deleted when you close your browser.


Personal Data

Delfin Educational Centre may process, inter alia, one or more types of the following personal data:

  • job applicants and Delfin Centre personnel, relating to their (potential) role within Delfin Centre including contact details, résumé, professional development, personnel file, benefits, compensation, etc.;
  • Delfin Centre employees’ family members and dependents, relating to employee benefits and services;
  • students which are being trained at Delfin Centre
  • consumers/members of the public, some of whom also become direct customers, relating to the services we provide to them or that they ask us about;
  • the personal data of the complainants, persons who entered in a written correspondence with a Dolfin Centre or deposited with documents containing their and / or foreign personal data and of other persons who, in any case, have contacted Delfin Centre in its capacity of a data controller,
  • images captured via CCTV cameras while Data Subject is within the Dolfin Centre's premises.
  • Other information collected on paper or electronically for visitors in the Delfin Centre`s premises.
  • Information about personal details of representatives of clients or customers.

Accuracy and Confidentiality

All Personal Data processed by Dolfin Educational Centre must be kept accurate, complete and up-to-date as is necessary for the Purpose(s) of Processing. The personnel of Delfin Centre shall make sure that the Personal Data obtained directly from Data Subjects or indirectly is verified against relevant documentation.


Delfin Educational Centre shall use appropriate technical and organizational measures to safeguard Personal Data, including when third parties are engaged in processing Personal Data on Delfin Centre’s behalf.

Personal Data Breach

All information/data breaches must be reported to the responsible privacy officer immediately as per the applicable requirements of the law.


Transfers of Personal Data

In the exercise of its business activities, Delfin Centre may transfer data with third parties only once it makes sure that data privacy and security is guaranteed appropriately.

When transferring Personal Data to a third party country outside the EU/EEA, Delfin Centre shall ensure that binding rules, in agreement or otherwise, require to the third party to provide adequate guarantees for the protection of Personal Data are in place.


Inserted content from other websites

Articles on this site can be embedded (videos, images, content, etc.). Embedded content from other sites accepts and treats the site visitor as a visitor to their own site.

These sites may collect information about you, use cookies, embed additional third-party statistics tools, monitor how you work with these tools, including how you use embedded content if you have an account and are logged into the site.

How long we hold your personal data?

If you leave a comment, the comment and its metadata are saved indefinitely. This is so that we can automatically recognize and approve your subsequent comments, instead of keeping them in the approval queue.

For users who register on our website (if any), we also store the personal information they provide in their user profile. All users can view, edit or delete their personal information at any time (except that they cannot change their username). Website administrators can view and edit this information.

Your rights over your personal data

If you have an account on this site or have left comments on it, you may request a file containing all the data we store about you, including data you have provided to us. You may also request that we delete the personal information we hold about you. However, this does not include data that we are required to store for administrative purposes, for the security of the site, or by law.

How we use your data?

Comments from visitors can be checked through an automatic spam comment check service.

How we protect your personal information?

Delfin Centre LTD restricts access to your personal data to its employees, except in cases where there are reasonable grounds to handle this information in order to perform their work duties. Delfin Center LTD has physical, electronic and procedural safeguards that comply with its legal obligations regarding the protection of information about you..
