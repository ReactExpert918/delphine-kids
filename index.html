Learn More About Us, Our Work And Our Cultural Activities üê¨

Dolphin Education Centre is a centre for language learning and personal development. Over the last 16 years, thanks to its team of expert teachers, more than 1000 children, in all age groups and levels, all over Bulgaria have received quality education, which helped them to fulfill their dreams.

The centre offers various activities related to foreign language courses in English, German, Italian, Spanish, Russian, Greek and Bulgarian for foreigners, as well as translation and legalization, and personal development courses.

We offer also a preparation for the Cambridge University English Certificate Levels exams, the Grade 12 English exam and the IELTS exam.

During the summer holidays from the beginning of June to the beginning of September, a Summer Day School for children from 5 to 11 years of age is held in the town of Petrich. The idea is to have fun by learning and exploring the world around us in a fun, interactive and building way, helping the personal growth of the younger generation!

In our program we have set on educational excursions for children in the region of Petrich and Bulgaria, hikes in the mountain Belasitsa together with the park Belasitsa - Petrich and two days a week beach to Rupite.
